Introducing The GGI Platform

Linking clients, our associates and leading-edge research capabilities and tools.

Who Is GGI?

Since its establishment in 1981, Goss Gilroy Inc. (GGI) has grown into one of the leading independent management consulting firms in Canada. Through our offices across Canada, our team of 40 professionals, and our growing network of over 100 associates, we deliver a wide range of consulting services to government, the non-profit sector and industry in each province and territory as well as internationally.

GGI Platform is a new initiative for us, but one that is firmly rooted in the way we’ve always done business. Most of our services involve team members from outside of the firm and, in fact, many of our projects are led by our Associates. What has changed is that we recognize this isn't just a preferred way of doing business, it is now an essential part of our business.

What is the GGI Platform?

Network of Associates with Leading Edge Tools

The GGI Platform brings together our growing network of Associates with leading edge management, communication, research and analytical tools, and strong marketing to create opportunities for our Associates and offer an ever-increasing range of high quality, leading-edge services for our clients.

Database of Associates

At the foundation of the GGI Platform is a searchable database that contains data on all of our associates. We draw from this database when developing effective project teams for our existing or proposed projects. We can also use this database to link our associates to clients who approach us as well as to other associates who are constructing teams for their projects.

Investment in Tools and Resources

To support our Associates, we will be making significant investments in leading edge management, communication, research and analytical tools and resources as well as marketing programs and tools. Over the next year, we will also be developing a series of new products and services that will incorporate services delivered by our associates and ourselves.

Why We Developed the GGI Platform

We established the GGI Platform as a means to respond to significant changes occuring in the consulting industry. Our research indicates that the industry will change more in the next five to ten years than it has in the past 40 years. For example:

Clients want specialists

Clients increasingly want teams of specialists, rather than generalists.
Consultants are under ever greater pressure to reduce timelines and costs.
New technologies have the potential to significantly improve how we market and deliver services, create project teams, and communicate with clients.

Growing our network of Associates, which enables us to quickly create project teams that feature the a wide range of skills needed by our clients.
Providing our clients and project teams with access to leading-edge management, communication, research and analytical tools.
Making significant investments in marketing and proposal development.
